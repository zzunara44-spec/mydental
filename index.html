About Us

Passionate About Your Dental Health

Over a decade of dedicated service to smiles across Hyderabad.

The Tooth Dental Studio was founded by Dr. Priya Sharma, a highly qualified dental professional with over 12 years of experience in comprehensive dental care. Holding a BDS from Osmania University and an MDS in Orthodontics from Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, Dr. Sharma brings expertise, compassion, and precision to every treatment.

Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional dental care in a welcoming, anxiety-free environment. We believe every patient deserves a healthy, confident smile — and we're here to help you achieve exactly that.
